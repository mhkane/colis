Airspress

A project led by five students from 4 universities:

Université Laval, École Polytechnique de Montréal, MIT and HEC Maroc.

It aims to offer a new model for shipping small quantities of items on long distance and enable people, professionals or businesses to take advantage of a dynamic travel-and-ship marketplace.
