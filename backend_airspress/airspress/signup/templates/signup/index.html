    ANYTHING, ANYWHERE

    The end of high shipping costs

    Have you ever thought of acquiring any item which is hard to obtain in your country of residence ?

    You probably didn’t buy it given the high cost of international shipping.

    With Airspress, we designed a platform which will enable us to reduce the cost of international shipping, together.

    Simple and effective

    As a buyer, you can place orders with travelers.

    As a traveler, Airspress will allow you to publish your trip information and be put in touch

    with people interested in placing orders with you in order to monetize the free space in your luggage.

    A whole opportunity...

    We are a peer-to-peer ordering platform harnessing the flow of travelers

    in order to simplify the process of acquiring items unavailable where you live.

    With Airspress, your trips visibility will increase and you will be able to better manage your orders

    and shopping commodities sold in other countries will become far easier and less onerous.
