What is the point of Airspress?

Airspress's goal is to simplify the process of ordering items difficult to obtain due to geographic constraints. We serve as a platform connecting customers willing to acquire an item outside of their country of residence and travelers who can bring it to them.

  How does it work ?

As a traveler, you will be able to register your trip information including the trip date, destination and an estimate of the available space in your suitcases. As a customer, you'll be able to be put in touch with travellers to request them to buy items for you. Once you get in contact with a traveler, you’ll be able to send him a request where you indicate various information about the item you want to order. Please keep in mind that ordering item which are easy to obtain in the traveler’s country of departure (through online shopping, duty-free or airport shop) will make things easier for him and make him more likely to offer you this service. After the receiving of the item, you and the traveler will have to confirm the request has been processed. We will offer a mutual rating system where travelers and purchasers will be able to give each other feedback.

  Who is Airspress for ?

The test version of Airspress is designed for international and exchange students and their acquaintances. This service allow them to ship items worldwide through the community of travelers. In the future, we plan to include more people in our community.

  How is Airspress different from traditional postal services ?

Airspress’s peer-to-peer approach allows to take advantage of complex flight itineraries. For example, let’s assume you are an Ivorian student in England and that you are going back to Abidjan this summer. Let’s also assume that one of your friend who is studying in New York is also coming back to Abidjan this summer but spends a couple days in Paris. With one Airspress order, you’ll be able to get items from both New York and Paris and receive them in Abidjan. The complexity of flight itineraries enables Airspress users to easily buy items from different places at a low cost which is very difficult to do with classical postal services.

  Why should I use AirSpress to receive/ship anything ?

As a buyer, some products are not easily accessible in your country and the current cost of international shipping make them even more out of reach. Airspress allow you to order and receive these products within days and for a low cost.

   Why, as a traveler, should I use Airspress ?

As a traveler, Airspress allow you to generate additional revenues by monetizing the space in your luggage as well as do a favor to someone. Doing so allows you to build your online reputation and join a community of people trying to spread the philosophy of the sharing economy.

  What about security ?

At Airspress, security is essential.
Our recommended use case in which the traveler receives the information about the product he is to acquire on behalf of the purchaser highlights this philosophy. In addition, email verification combined with our review and confirmation system allows you to keep track of your order and also assess the reliability of the person you interact with. That said, we are open to suggestion and always look to make this service more reliable.

 Which goods are allowed to be transported by an Airspress user ?

Airspress exists under the laws of the countries where its services are used. All goods(food, textile, electronic devices...etc.) are allowed unless otherwise specified by law in said countries and according to the context specified by those laws.

 How can you keep contact with the traveler and make sure he gets you your order ?

Airspress has a chat feature allowing you to reliably communicate with the traveler.

  How to make sure i'm meeting the right person when delivering ?

In case you are to meet physically, each user can have a profile picture allowing you to identify him/her. Also feel free to use "secret words" (or anything similar) that you'd have devised with the buyer.

  How much does it cost ?

When a trip is published, the traveler decides on his margin; so it is up to them to decide how much they will charge while keeping in mind that their price should stay competitive with other international shipping services.
